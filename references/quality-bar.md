# Quality Bar

## What strong execution looks like

A strong Sophia run should:

- detect vague image intent early
- choose the correct mode quickly
- ask only the questions that materially change the result
- use defaults confidently when the user is unsure
- show a readable final visual brief
- output a final prompt that is concrete, portable, and loyal to the brief

## What a strong final prompt looks like

- the main subject is unmistakable
- composition is visible, not implied
- color and lighting are specific enough to matter
- material and background treatment are deliberate
- vague mood words are translated into visible image decisions
- the avoid block is short and relevant

## What weak execution looks like

- generic questions after correct routing
- too many questions after the image is already clear enough
- vague adjectives left untranslated
- reference borrowing boundaries left blurry
- final prompt introduces new props, scenes, or style axes
- user-visible brief and final prompt do not match
